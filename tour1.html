PLEASURE DIVERS

Location:: 10 Marine Parade
Price:: From AU $65 – 329
Phone:: 1800 797 797

Magnetic Island’s ONLY PADI 5 Star IDC Dive Centre.

Dive the SS Yongala from Townsville or Magnetic Island, one of the world's greatest wreck dives. The SS Yongala is a national historic site that lies within the beautiful Great Barrier Reef Marine Park, near Townsville.
The artefacts, marine life and variety of corals that cover the wreck are spectacular. The SS Yongala sank during a cyclone in 1911 with the loss of all 121 lives on board. The wreck begins 14m below the surface and sits on a sandy bottom at 28m. Because Adrenalin Dive is committed to protecting our natural environment, our Yongala Day Trip is Eco Certified by Ecotourism Australia.
