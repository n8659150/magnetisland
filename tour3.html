SEA KAYAKS

Location:: Horseshoe Bay
Price:: $85 per person
Phone:: (07) 4778 5424

Discover Magnetic Island’s World Heritage National and Marine Parks from the quiet of your kayak.

Magnetic Island Sea Kayaks is one of the oldest running operations on the east coast established in 1993. Its owner Steve is an Australian Canoeing Advanced Level 3 sea kayak Guide, Instructor and Assessor who has been kayaking for over 35 years, teaching and guiding around the world for over 25 years.Enjoy a tropical breakfast on a secluded picturesque beach, viewing nature in the wild, where we take only photos and leave only footprints. No experience necessary as full instruction is provided.

Morning Tour:

Discover Magnetic Island’s National and Marine Parks from the quiet of your kayak. Enjoy a tropical breakfast on a secluded picturesque beach, viewing nature in the wild, where we take only photos and leave only footprints. No experience necessary as full instruction is provided.

Sunset Tour:

Take a leisurely paddle in the calm waters of Horseshoe Bay, enjoy a drink and watch the sunset behind anchored sail boats

Kayak rentals also available from $60 per day.
